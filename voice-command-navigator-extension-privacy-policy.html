Voice Command Navigator Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-18

Overview

Voice Command Navigator is a browser extension that lets users navigate tabs, scroll pages, click links, and optionally dictate text using voice commands.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. It does not collect browsing history, page contents, or identifiers.

Data Storage

The extension stores only local preferences (such as always-on voice and dictation mode state) in Chrome storage on your device.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.
